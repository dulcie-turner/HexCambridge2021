About Us



What is Mindful Calendar?


Mindful Calendar is a website designed to help you stay organised and on top of work, while also providing you with tools to help prevent burnout. Reaching a state of burnout is easily done in today's hustle culture but can take a long time to recover from.



How does it work?


Simply fill in your daily task list, put them onto your calendar then mindful calendar will provide feedback on your workload and suggest some activities that fit into your schedule, with the aim to prevent burnout.
